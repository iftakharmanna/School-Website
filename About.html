ABOUT US

Shammi Child Academy is a private school located in Bagha Daulatpur, Golapgonj, Sylhet. Founded in 2003, the school serves students from Play-Group to class 5. Our staff goes well beyond the curriculum mandates. We offer extra curricular activities such as Independence day celebrations, Eid gatherings, class picnics, parties, etc.

SCHOOL MISSION

Our mission is to promote lifelong learning by nurturing academic excellence, good character, and appreciation of traditions.
